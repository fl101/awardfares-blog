In November 2023, the aviation industry is witnessing the introduction of several new routes, particularly in the Asian and American markets. Star Alliance members, such as LOT Polish Airlines, are expanding their reach. LOT is launching a new connection from Wroclaw to Seoul, and Lufthansa is also enhancing its Asian network by starting flights from Munich to Bengaluru, further connecting India and Germany. 

In the United States, Turkish Airlines is beginning a new service from Istanbul to Detroit, continuing its expansion in the American market. In the Australian market, United Airlines is inaugurating a route from Los Angeles to Brisbane. Additionally, South African Airways is using their A330s on two new routes from Capetown and Johannesburg to São Paulo, Brazil's financial capital.

Outside of Star Alliance, there are also various airlines kicking off international services, including new flights to Australia by carriers such as Batik Air and Vietjet, Norse Atlantic Airways establishing a link between Oslo and Bangkok, and more.
